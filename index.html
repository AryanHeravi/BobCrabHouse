About Us

"We're Not Fancy, We Care About Quality"

Our Story

Babak (Bob) purchased this building in the northside of Jacksonville with a vision of serving the community Crab & Seafood.

Since 2009, Bob has been providing excellent customer service, and displaying his knowledge of Crab & Seafood to his customers.

About Bob

Bob (Babak) was born in 1972, in Iran. He moved to America as a refugee in 2001. He worked multiple jobs until he found his passion for crab and seafood. In 2009, Bob opened up Bob's Crab House.
